Privacy Policy
for Odia KidsApp
Last Updated: September 2, 2025
Our Commitment to Privacy
Odia KidsApp is designed especially for children and families. We take privacy very seriously and ensure a safe, secure, and ad-free experience.
Information Collection
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app's core features work entirely offline and it does not ask for names, emails, phone numbers, photos, or location data.
Third-Party Services & Ads
This app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising networks. It contains no ads and does not send promotional push notifications from a server. It offers an optional, user-enabled local reminder feature, which is described in more detail below.
Permissions and Device Features
Some menu options, like "Rate App", "Share", or "WhatsApp Support", may open external apps or websites. All such actions are protected by a parental gate to prevent children from leaving the app without adult approval.

The app requests the INTERNET permission solely for these parent-gated features. The app's core functionality is completely offline, and no data is transmitted otherwise.

Additionally, the app may request the Notification permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS). This permission is required only if you choose to enable the optional daily reminder feature, either on the settings screen or when opening the app for the first time.
Optional Daily Reminders
The app includes an optional feature to set a daily reminder notification. These notifications are scheduled and managed entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers. This feature is turned off by default and must be manually enabled by a parent in the app's settings screen or when opening the app for the first time. You can disable these reminders at any time from the same menu.
Children's Privacy
We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy to ensure a safe experience for all our young users.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
