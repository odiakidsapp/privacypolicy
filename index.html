Privacy Policy
for Odia KidsApp
Last Updated: November 6, 2025
Our Commitment to Privacy
Odia KidsApp is designed especially for children and families. We take privacy very seriously and ensure a safe, secure, and ad-free experience.
Information Collection
We do not collect, store, or share any *personally identifiable information* like names, emails, phone numbers, photos, or location data. The app's core features work entirely offline and it does not ask for such data.
In-App Purchases & Purchase Verification
Odia KidsApp offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features. This transaction is processed securely by the Google Play Store.

We do not collect, see, or store any personal payment details (like credit card numbers). All payment transactions are handled securely by Google.

To verify your premium status and restore your purchase, our app communicates with Google Play's service. This process may involve Google sharing "User Identifiers" (anonymized Google Account IDs) and "Purchase History" data with the app. This information is used *only* for the purpose of unlocking your premium content and is not stored or shared by us for any other reason.

For more information, please review the Google Payments Privacy Notice.
Third-Party Services & Ads
This app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising networks. It contains no ads. The only third-party service used is Google Play's own Billing Library to process and verify purchases, as described above.
Permissions and Device Features
Some menu options, like “Rate App”, “Share”, or “WhatsApp Support”, may open external apps or websites. All such actions are protected by a parental gate to prevent children from leaving the app without adult approval.

The app requests the INTERNET permission. This is required for parent-gated features and for communicating with the Google Play Store to make and verify in-app purchases. The app's core educational functionality is completely offline.
Children’s Privacy
We comply with the Google Play Families Policy and COPPA guidelines to ensure a safe experience for children. All purchase options are located behind a parental gate.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
