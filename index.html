Privacy Policy
for Odia KidsApp
Last Updated: April 17, 2026
Our Commitment to Privacy
Odia KidsApp is designed especially for children and families. We take privacy very seriously and ensure a safe, secure, and ad-free experience.
Information Collection & Tracking
We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information like names, emails, phone numbers, photos, or location data. Furthermore, we do not use any hidden trackers or device identifiers to profile your child or track their activity across other apps.
Google Play Services (Billing, Updates & Reviews)
Odia KidsApp uses official Google Play Core libraries to provide essential app functions:

1. In-App Purchases: Processed securely by the Google Play Billing Library. We do not see or store payment details.

2. In-App Updates: Used to provide critical software fixes and performance improvements.

3. In-App Reviews: Used to allow parents to provide feedback via the Google Play Store.

These services may involve Google sharing anonymized account identifiers to verify your premium status or facilitate system prompts. No personal data is transmitted to us during these processes.
Third-Party Services & Ads
This app contains no ads and does not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. The only external libraries used are the official Google Play Service SDKs required for the features described above.
Permissions and Device Features
The app requests INTERNET permission only for parent-authorized actions, including:

• Processing and verifying in-app purchases.
• Checking for mandatory software updates.
• Facilitating optional app reviews via the Play Store.

All actions that require an internet connection are protected by a parental gate. The app’s core educational functionality works completely offline.
Children’s Privacy & Parental Gates
We comply strictly with the Google Play Families Policy and COPPA guidelines. To ensure a safe experience, all 'adult' features—including the settings menu, in-app purchases, software updates, app ratings, and external links—are located behind a secure parental gate (math problem).
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
